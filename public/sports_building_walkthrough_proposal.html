Project Overview

Riva Studios is pleased to present this proposal for developing an interactive 3D walkthrough of your sports building. Our goal is to create an immersive, high-fidelity experience that integrates seamlessly into your website, allowing visitors to explore the sports facilities on desktop, mobile, and VR headsets.

View the proof-of-concept here: shiva3dx.github.io/DEMO/ (Supports desktop keyboard walking, mobile touch dragging, and immersive VR mode).

Pricing Packages

To provide flexibility for your budget, we offer two implementation options:

Recommended
Option 1: 360° Photorealistic Panorama Tour
Users navigate between locations (e.g. lobby, gym, court) by clicking responsive hotspots. Look around 360° with smooth transitions.
  • Visual Quality: Photorealistic rendering (baked offline using Blender Cycles).
  • Static Deliverables: **10 High-Resolution Rendered Images** for print/marketing use.
  • Loading Performance: Ultra-fast load times (<2 seconds), optimized for mobile networks.
  • VR Support: Fully compatible with WebXR (Meta Quest, Apple Vision Pro) directly in browser.
  • Timeline: 3–4 Days
₹30,000.00 INR (Exclusive of GST)
Option 2: Interactive Real-Time 3D Walkthrough
A video-game style walkthrough allowing users to walk freely inside the 3D model using WASD keys or click-to-move with full physics and collision.
  • Visual Quality: High (real-time graphics running dynamically in browser).
  • Static Deliverables: **10 High-Resolution Rendered Images** for print/marketing use.
  • Navigation: Continuous free-roaming, collision detection (prevents passing through walls).
  • Optimization: Re-topologizing and decimation of the client's 1.5 GB model down to ~25MB.
  • Timeline: 7–10 Days
₹75,000.00 INR (Exclusive of GST)
